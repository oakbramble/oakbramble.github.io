Rebecca Allen

Fantasy improviser, game master and fitness trainer

After training and performing improv theatre for some years, I discovered table top role playing games at the start of 2020. I would say it was love at first roll, but even before my first game I knew RPGs were for me.

I jumped straight into game mastering and now run a combination of D&D campaigns and public meetups for totally improvised one shots (mostly based on one page games from Grant Howitt).

In autumn 2023 I will start running a group that meets to build improv muscles for RPG players and GMs.

My love of performing and energising groups is also given an outlet in my work as a group fitness trainer.

Having had very negative encounters with sport as a child and in school, I would never have imagined being in this position. However with the joy and strength I now feel from powering through workouts, and the very tangible changes that have followed in body and mind, I now firmly believe that anyone can get fit at any age and, importantly, that everyone is allowed to get fit. Sport is not just for those who were sporty at school.
